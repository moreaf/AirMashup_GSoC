Model name: EMBRAER EJR FAMILY

Development and production

The Embraer ERJ family (or Embraer Regional Jet, model name EMB 135 and EMB 145) is a series of
twin-engine regional jets produced by Embraer, a Brazilian aerospace company. Aircraft in the series
include the ERJ135 (37 passengers), ERJ140 (44 passengers), and ERJ145 (50 passengers), as well
as the Legacy business jet and the R-99 family of military aircraft. Each jet in the series is powered by
two turbofan engines. The family's primary competition comes from the Bombardier CRJ regional jets.
